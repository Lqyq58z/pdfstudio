Common Questions

  • How do I restore purchases? Open the app, go to Profile / Membership Center, then tap Restore Purchases.
  • Where are processed files saved? Processed files are stored in the app's Documents area and can be shared or exported through iOS system sharing options.
  • Are my documents uploaded? Most document processing is performed locally on your device. Your files are not uploaded to our own server.
  • Why is a tool limited? Free users have daily local tool limits. VIP membership unlocks additional usage and premium features.

App Features

PDF全能编辑 supports PDF merging, splitting, compression, signing, scanning, page operations, watermarking, password protection, PDF reading, PDF annotations, image to PDF, Word/PPT to PDF, text utilities, and document history.
